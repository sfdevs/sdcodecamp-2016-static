Kris Boedigheimer

Bio:

I am a former computer programmer turned homeschool mom who loves learning, travel, exercise, reading, finding exciting new hobbies, and spending time with family. My favorite subjects are programming, math, and logic. Besides teaching homeschool I am a third degree black belt in Tae Kwon Do, Advanced Level Toastmaster Speaker, and group fitness instructor for Les Mills Bodyflow, and Les Milles BodyAttack. We travel constantly and make it a habit to try something different at each location including blown glass, painting classes, surfing, carving carousel animals, and trapeze class.

Session(s):

  • Top Ten Ways to Teach Your Kids to Code

    Find out the best ways to teach your kids how to code. You will learn tactical ways to show the idea of programming, check out some of the apps and programs available, and learn strategies for making it fun. We will look at puzzles, games, apps, curriculum, websites and more. Learn how to engage your child, and keep them interested in coding. From games like Robot Turtles and Code Monkey Island to hardware and robots with Raspberry Pi and Sphero. Prepare to be educated, energized and ready to show your kids why you chose software as a profession.
