About Us

South Dakota Code Camp is The Mount Rushmore State's largest developer conference. We are an annual event held in Sioux Falls, SD. The event is completely free, covered entirely by our gracious sponsors.

South Dakota Code Camp follows the guidelines published in the Code Camp manifesto. We expect our attendees to adhere to our code of conduct policy.

As this is a community-driven event, we are always looking for people to lead sessions, volunteer to help during the day of the event, etc. If you'd like to participate, or if you'd like to contribute to the event, let us know.
