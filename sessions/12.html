Lions, Tigers and ORMs, Oh My!

Presented by: Ivan Ven Osdel

A light-hearted exploration of the magical and sometimes scary world of Object Relational Mappers, this session begins with an overview contrasting various libraries, and ends with a deep dive into one ORM library in particular. With this talk I hope to break down the utility of an ORM, as well as when and how it's appropriate to employ one.
